Unity run to Tribal Heritage Site

Posted On: 31 oct 2024 | 08:39 AM

In celebration of Rashtriya Ekta Diwas (National Unity Day), the students of EMRS Dornala organized a spirited Unity Run through the heart of the tribal areas, spreading the message of national integration and harmony. With banners, chants, and unwavering energy, the students symbolized the strength of unity in diversity. The event not only fostered community engagement but also highlighted the active role of EMRS in nurturing responsible and patriotic youth among tribal populations. "Unity in diversity is our strength — and today, our students carried that message with pride and purpose." — Principal, EMRS Dornala

Marking National Unity Day, students of EMRS Dornala took to the streets in a symbolic run across nearby tribal regions. The event aimed to strengthen national pride and solidarity, especially among tribal communities. With energetic slogans and a vibrant display of cultural unity, the students left a powerful impact on local residents, promoting the values of peace, brotherhood, and national integrity. The Unity Run reflects our students’ commitment to building a stronger, united India — starting from their own roots. — EMRS Staff Coordinator

ISRO Inspires EMRS Dornala Learners Towards a Scientific Future

Posted On: 15 October 2023 | 10:15 AM

A group of enthusiastic students from EMRS Dornala recently visited the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO), gaining firsthand insight into India’s space missions and satellite technology. The educational tour sparked curiosity and ambition among the tribal students, many of whom dream of contributing to the nation’s space journey. The interaction with scientists and exposure to real-time space models inspired the next generation of thinkers and innovators.

“This visit made me believe that even we, from remote areas, can aim for the stars.” — A student participant

Students of EMRS Dornala embarked on an eye-opening educational tour to ISRO, where they learned about satellite launches, space technology, and India’s space achievements. The visit aimed to motivate tribal youth to pursue STEM careers and contribute to the country's scientific development. It was a proud and transformative experience that broadened their vision beyond textbooks.

“This trip has planted the seeds of scientific curiosity in our students,” — Science Faculty, EMRS Dornala

Checkmate! EMRS Dornala Promotes Mental Discipline through Chess

Posted On: 28 September 2023 | 06:45 PM

Students of EMRS Dornala are actively engaging in the game of chess, sharpening their strategic thinking and problem-solving skills. The school has introduced regular chess sessions as part of co-curricular activities, encouraging students from tribal backgrounds to explore intellectual sports. With growing interest and discipline, students are learning to think ahead, stay calm under pressure, and develop patience and focus.

“Chess teaches us to make the right move in life, not just on the board.” — Student Participant

The chess initiative at EMRS Dornala is gaining momentum as students show remarkable enthusiasm and talent in this classic mind game. Regular in-house matches and training sessions are helping students boost their memory, logic, and decision-making skills. Teachers report increased concentration and confidence among players, making chess an essential part of student development.

“In every match, our students are not just playing — they are growing.” — EMRS Sports Coordinator

Tradition Takes the Stage: EMRS Dornala Students Shine in Folk Dance Performances

Posted On: 12 August 2023 | 11:30 AM

In a colorful celebration of tribal traditions, students of EMRS Dornala performed breathtaking cultural dances, showcasing the rich heritage of their communities. Dressed in traditional attire, they brought alive the rhythms and stories passed down through generations. The event highlighted the school’s dedication to preserving indigenous art while nurturing pride in cultural identity.

“Through every step and beat, our students expressed the soul of their roots.” — Cultural Incharge, EMRS Dornala

Students of EMRS Dornala lit up the stage with their traditional dance performances, representing the beauty and unity of tribal cultures. From tribal drum beats to graceful movements, the performances celebrated diversity and tradition. The event fostered a deep sense of belonging and encouraged students to honor and pass on their ancestral legacy.

“This cultural dance was not just a performance — it was a proud echo of our history.” — Student Performer

Spiritual Vibes Fill EMRS Dornala as Students Celebrate Ganesh Chaturthi 2024

Posted On: 7th sept 2024| 09:00 AM

On 7th September 2024, EMRS Dornala celebrated Ganesh Chaturthi with great enthusiasm and spiritual fervor. Students and staff came together to welcome Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, with traditional rituals, devotional songs, and cultural performances. A beautifully crafted idol was installed on campus, and eco-friendly practices were followed throughout the celebration. The event instilled values of unity, faith, and environmental awareness among the students.

“Celebrating Ganpati Bappa together brought a divine energy to our campus,” — EMRS Cultural Committee

On 7th September 2024, the campus of EMRS Dornala echoed with chants of "Ganpati Bappa Morya!" as students and staff celebrated Ganesh Chaturthi with deep devotion and cultural spirit. The celebration featured a traditional aarti, student-led bhajans, and a cultural dance dedicated to Lord Ganesha. The eco-friendly idol immersion and collective prayer reflected the school’s commitment to values, culture, and sustainability.

“The celebration was a perfect blend of tradition, learning, and joy,” — Principal, EMRS Dornala

Eco-Friendly Campus Initiative

Posted On: 05 June 2023 | 08:00 AM

EMRS Dornala launched a comprehensive environmental program on World Environment Day, combining modern ecology with traditional tribal conservation practices.

Students planted over 200 native saplings around the campus, with tribal elders explaining the traditional uses and significance of each species. The school inaugurated a waste management system incorporating tribal methods of recycling and composting.

A special workshop demonstrated how tribal communities traditionally lived in harmony with nature, offering lessons for sustainable living today. Students created an "Eco-Map" of the campus identifying areas for conservation and renewable energy projects.

This initiative reflects EMRS's vision of education that connects modern environmental science with indigenous ecological knowledge.
