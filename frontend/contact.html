Visiting Hours & Guidelines

Parent Visitation Policy

We welcome parents to visit the school and meet their wards following these guidelines:

  • Regular visiting days: 2nd and 4th Sunday of each month
  • Visiting hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  • Prior appointment required for meeting teachers or principal
  • Maximum 2 visitors per student allowed
  • Valid photo ID mandatory for all visitors

For emergency visits outside scheduled hours, please contact the hostel warden in advance.
