About EMRS Dornala

Our journey, vision, and mission

Our Story

Established in 2010 under the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, Government of India, EMRS Dornala was created with the vision of providing quality education to tribal children in a residential setting. Located in the picturesque Prakasam district of Andhra Pradesh, our school has grown from humble beginnings to become a center of educational excellence.

Our Vision

To empower tribal youth through holistic education that preserves their cultural identity while equipping them with the knowledge, skills, and values to thrive in a global society.

Our Mission

  • Provide free, quality residential education to tribal students from Class VI to XII
  • Offer a balanced curriculum combining academic excellence with cultural preservation
  • Create a nurturing environment that fosters intellectual, physical, and emotional growth
  • Develop leadership qualities and social responsibility among students
  • Bridge the gap between tribal communities and mainstream opportunities

Management Perspective

Leadership that guides our institution

Message from the Chairman

Shri. Venkateswara Rao

Chairman, EMRS Governing Council

"At EMRS Dornala, we believe that education is the most powerful tool for tribal empowerment. Our institution is committed to providing a transformative educational experience that combines academic rigor with cultural sensitivity.

We take pride in creating an environment where tribal children can excel without losing touch with their roots. The residential nature of our school ensures that students receive round-the-clock guidance and support, helping them develop into well-rounded individuals.

Our focus extends beyond textbooks to include life skills, leadership development, and community engagement. I invite you to explore what makes EMRS Dornala a unique and nurturing space for tribal youth to learn, grow, and succeed."

Academic Perspective

Excellence in teaching and learning

Message from the Principal

Sh. Ishwar Singh Bhardwaj

Principal, EMRS Dornala

"As the Principal of EMRS Dornala, it is my privilege to lead a team of dedicated educators who are committed to unlocking the potential of every student. Our academic program is designed to challenge and inspire, while providing the support needed for success.

Affiliation & Recognition

Our credentials and partnerships

CBSE Affiliation

EMRS Dornala is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi (Affiliation No: 123456). Our students appear for the All India Secondary School Examination (Class X) and All India Senior School Certificate Examination (Class XII).

We follow the NCERT curriculum with additional emphasis on tribal culture and local environmental studies. Our academic calendar aligns with CBSE guidelines while accommodating local festival schedules.

Visit CBSE Website

NESTS

As part of the National Education Society for Tribal Students (NESTS), we benefit from centralized academic support, teacher training programs, and resource sharing with other EMRS schools across India.

NESTS provides guidelines for uniform quality standards across all EMRS institutions while allowing flexibility to address local needs and cultural contexts.
