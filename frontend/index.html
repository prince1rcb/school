About EMRS Dornala

Nurturing Excellence Since Our Inception

EMRS Dornala stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh. We are committed to providing quality education to tribal students, fostering their intellectual, emotional, and social development.
