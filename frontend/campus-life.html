Safety & Security

Our commitment to student wellbeing

24/7 Security

Trained security personnel, CCTV surveillance, and controlled entry/exit points ensure campus safety round the clock.

Medical Care

On-campus infirmary with qualified nurse and tie-ups with nearby hospitals for emergencies. Regular health check-ups conducted.

Child Protection

Strict anti-bullying policy with trained counselors and grievance redressal system. All staff undergo background checks.

Disaster Preparedness

Regular fire drills, earthquake preparedness training, and emergency response protocols in place.

School Rules & Discipline

Guidelines for a harmonious community

General Conduct

  • Respect for all members of the school community is mandatory
  • Regular attendance and punctuality are expected
  • School uniform must be worn properly during school hours
  • Use of mobile phones restricted to designated times and areas
  • Damage to school property will result in disciplinary action

Academic Expectations

  • Completion of all assignments and projects on time
  • Active participation in classroom activities
  • Maintenance of notebooks and study materials
  • Honesty in academic work (zero tolerance for cheating)

Residential Rules

  • Strict adherence to hostel timings and curfews
  • Rooms must be kept clean and organized
  • Visitors require prior permission
  • Lights out at prescribed time
  • Group study sessions encouraged

Disciplinary Measures

  • Verbal warning for minor infractions
  • Written notice to parents for repeated issues
  • Probation for serious violations
  • Suspension or expulsion for grave misconduct

Student Chapters & Clubs

Opportunities for leadership and exploration

Science Club

Explore scientific concepts through experiments, projects, and competitions. Members participate in state and national science fairs.

Arts & Culture

Celebrate tribal heritage through painting, dance, music, and drama. Annual cultural fest showcases student talents.

Eco Club

Promote environmental awareness through tree planting, recycling initiatives, and sustainability projects.

Literary Society

Develop language skills through debates, elocution, creative writing, and publishing the school magazine.

Sports Council

Organize inter-house competitions, train for tournaments, and promote fitness through various sports activities.

Social Service

Engage in community service projects, village outreach programs, and awareness campaigns on social issues.
